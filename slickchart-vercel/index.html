Good questions

Frequently asked.

What is SlickChart?

SlickChart is a client charting and engagement app for independent beauty, health, and wellness professionals. It keeps your client notes, before and after photos, forms, invoicing, and reports in one place, right from your phone.

Who is SlickChart for?

SlickChart is built for solo providers like estheticians, tattoo artists, massage therapists, nail techs, lash and brow artists, hair stylists, nutritionists, and health and wellness coaches.

Do I need Square to use SlickChart?

No. SlickChart works beautifully alongside Square, so your bookings, catalog, and payments flow right in. If you do not use Square, everything still works on its own.

How much does SlickChart cost?

SlickChart is $10 a month, and every feature is included with no higher tiers.

Do my clients pay anything?

Never. Your clients get the full experience: visit summaries, forms, messaging, rebooking, and their own progress photos, all completely free. It is all covered by your $10 a month subscription.

Do my clients need to download an app?

No, and that never changes. You send any client their visit summary, aftercare, forms, or product picks straight to their phone by text or email, and it opens right in their browser with nothing to install and no account to create. Even once SlickChart is in the app stores, that app store version is just an optional convenience for you and other providers, your clients keep using their private link with no download, ever.

Can I turn off the AI features?

Yes. Every AI feature, including voice notes and summaries, has a simple on and off switch. Turn it all off and SlickChart works exactly the same, just without the assist.

What can I do to protect my practice?

SlickChart sends, signs, and stores digital consent and liability waivers, and its intake forms can flag contraindications before a client ever walks in.
