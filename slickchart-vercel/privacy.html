Privacy Policy

SlickChart · Last updated July 9, 2026

SlickChart is a tool your skincare, beauty, or wellness provider uses to share your care with you — visit summaries, aftercare, forms, appointments, and messages. This policy explains what information the app handles and the choices you have.

Who this is between

When you use your personal SlickChart link, you have a private connection with one provider — the professional who invited you. Your information is used to support that care relationship. It is shared with your provider and is never sold or used for advertising.

Information the app handles

  • Contact details — your name, email, and phone number (entered by you or your provider).
  • Care information — notes, treatment summaries, aftercare, and intake/consent forms your provider shares with you or that you complete.
  • Photos you choose to upload — for check-ins, virtual consults, chat messages, or your profile picture.
  • Appointments & reminders — your upcoming visit and reminder preferences, including your device's time zone so reminders arrive at a sensible local time.
  • Notification settings — whether you've turned on notifications and, if so, a push subscription for your device so reminders and messages can reach you.
  • Basic technical data — standard information needed to load and secure the app.

How your information is used

  • To show you your summaries, aftercare, forms, and appointments.
  • To let you and your provider message each other and share photos.
  • To send reminders and notifications you've asked for (you can turn these off any time).

How it's shared

Your information is shared only with your provider. We also rely on a small number of trusted service providers to run the app — for website hosting and the secure database, and to send emails such as your invite link. These providers process data only to operate SlickChart and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

Notifications

If you turn on notifications, the app registers your device to receive push messages (for example a new message from your provider or an appointment reminder). You can turn notifications off at any time in Profile → Notifications, or in your device settings.

Security & your link

Your data is protected in transit, and your personal space is reached through a unique, hard-to-guess link. Please keep your link private — anyone with it can see your information. If you think your link has been shared, ask your provider to reissue it.

Your choices & deleting your data

You can delete your data from within the app at any time: Profile → Privacy & data → Delete my data. This removes your saved information from the app and notifies your provider. Because your provider is the custodian of your treatment records, they may be required by professional or legal obligations to retain certain records for a period of time; contact your provider directly with any questions about their retention.

Children

SlickChart is not directed to children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their information. Minors should use the app only with a parent or guardian's involvement.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
