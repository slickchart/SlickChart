## Full description (Apple 4000 / Play 4000 char max)

SlickChart is the client record built for solo beauty and wellness professionals — estheticians, tattoo artists, brow & PMU specialists, hair and barbers, and more. Keep every client's history, photos, forms, and follow-ups in one place, and give your clients a beautiful private app of their own.

WHY PROVIDERS LOVE IT
• One client record — notes, photos, forms, products, and history, always with you.
• Before/after photos with side-by-side compare, angle tags, and progress tracking.
• Digital intake & consent forms clients sign on their phone — flagged contraindications surface before the visit.
• Session summaries and homecare routines you send straight to your client's app.
• Pre-visit check-ins so you walk into every appointment prepared.

WORKS WITH SQUARE
Connect your existing Square account and SlickChart keeps in sync — bookings, catalog, inventory, retail purchase history, cards on file, loyalty, refunds, and gift-card balances. No double entry.

A CLIENT APP THEY'LL ACTUALLY OPEN
Each client gets a private link to their own SlickChart: their next appointment, aftercare and homecare routine, session summaries, recommended products, and secure messaging with you.

SMART, OPTIONAL AI
Generate a quick pre-visit brief or a clean session summary, and dictate notes by voice. AI features are optional and can be turned off anytime.

BUILT FOR TRUST
Your data syncs securely to the cloud so nothing lives on just one device. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time.

Perfect for solo providers and small studios who want to look polished, save time, and give clients a premium experience — without a clunky, expensive system.

*SlickChart is a practice-management tool, not a medical device, and does not provide medical advice.*

> ⚠️ **Payments note:** SlickChart's own subscription is billed through Stripe/your website, and Client
> payments run through the provider's Square account. Apple/Google generally require **their** in-app
> purchase system for *digital* subscriptions sold in the app. If your subscription is sold or upgraded
> inside the app, review the IAP rules — you may need to (a) only let existing web subscribers sign in,
> not subscribe in-app, or (b) add Apple/Google in-app purchase for the subscription. Physical
> services/products (salon services, retail) are exempt and can use Square. Worth confirming before you
> submit so review doesn't bounce it.
